In this experiment, you take on the role of a CEO of a hypothetical company that wants to develop a product. If you manage to get a new product, you will receive {{ Constants.reward}} points.

You have to decide how much you want to invest in reasearch and development for this potential new product. However, the positive effect of aditional investments in reasearch and development decreases the more you invest, i.e. the difference between investing 10 and 20 points is greater then the difference between investing 80 and 90 points.

You will make {{ Constants.num_rounds}} decisions in total, and you can use the outcome of the previous decisions to help you figure out what the optimal level of investment is. The proces is not deterministic, the same level of investment will often lead to different outcomes. And the outcome of each decision does not depend on how much you invested in previous periods.

Once the experiment is over you will be paid {{Constants.dollars_per_point}} $ per point.
